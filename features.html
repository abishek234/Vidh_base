Core Platform Features

Comprehensive tools designed to streamline every aspect of school administration and enhance the learning experience.

Voice-Controlled Grading System

Revolutionary mark entry using natural speech recognition. Teachers can input grades by simply speaking roll numbers and marks, with automatic grade calculation and instant result generation.

Single-Click Attendance Management

Effortless attendance tracking for daily and special classes with just one click. Streamlined interface allows teachers to mark attendance for entire classes in seconds.

Geofencing Teacher Attendance

Location-based teacher check-in and check-out system ensuring accurate attendance tracking with geographical verification and automated reporting.

AI-Integrated Fee Management

Smart fee notifications, bulk structure management, automated receipt generation, and AI-powered reminders for seamless fee collection and tracking.

Intelligent School Calendar

Easy holiday configuration, Saturday settings, working day determination, and automated schedule management to streamline school planning.

Digital Homework & Notices

Comprehensive homework assignment system with interactive noticeboard featuring polling, comments, and bookmark functionality for important announcements.
