How Vidhyarthika Works for You

Experience the power of AI-integrated school management with seamless workflows designed for administrators, teachers, students, and parents.

Single Click Attendance Management System

Effortless attendance tracking for daily and special classes with just one click, streamlining the entire attendance process for teachers.

Voice Based Grading System

Revolutionary voice-controlled mark entry using roll number and mark format, with automated grade calculation and instant results.
